About Us

PoultryDetect is an AI-powered platform built to help poultry farmers identify and manage common poultry diseases with speed and accuracy. Using advanced image recognition technology and deep learning models like ResNet and VGG, we provide real-time disease detection based on visual symptoms.

Our goal is to promote healthier poultry farming by enabling early intervention, reducing losses, and improving overall flock health. Whether you are a small-scale farmer or a commercial poultry operator, PoultryDetect helps you make data-driven decisions with ease.
